What to look for. Green GB tags should appear on every card in both columns — if any show red non-GB, the countryCode fix has a gap. Red date pills mark events inside 48 hours: a column full of them is the “next few days, little inventory” problem. Amber tags show the TM price floor — genuinely in-demand events usually have one.

Caveat, stated up front: TM's relevance ranking is designed to score results against a keyword. With no keyword supplied its behaviour is undocumented and may simply be arbitrary. If the two columns come back near-identical, that is the likely reason, and the real fix is a wider date window plus our own ranking rather than a TM sort value.
